The Bulldog, also known as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog, is a medium-sized dog breed. It is a muscular, hefty dog with a wrinkled face and a distinctive pushed-in nose.

The Dobermann, or Doberman Pinscher in the United States and Canada, is a medium-large breed of domestic dog that was originally developed around 1890 by Karl Friedrich Louis Dobermann, a tax collector from Germany. The Dobermann has a long muzzle. It stands on its pads and is not usually heavy-footed. Dobermanns are known to be intelligent, alert, and tenaciously loyal companions and guard dogs.

The Pug has a compact, well-built body. The body of this dog is considered well-proportioned if the height up to the shoulders is close to the length from shoulder to bottom. Their skin is soft and comfortable to touch. They have round, dark convex eyes and a slightly awkward lower jaw. The tail is straight or twisted. Proper care of the wrinkles is of utmost importance.

Ranked second most intelligent dog breed just behind the Border Collie, the poodle is skillful in many dog sports and activities, including agility, obedience, tracking, herding, circus performance, and assistance dogs. Poodles have taken top honors in many conformation shows.

The Alaskan Malamute is a large breed of dog that was originally bred for their strength and endurance to haul heavy freight as a sled dog and hound. They are similar to other arctic, husky, and spitz breeds such as the Greenland Dog, Canadian Eskimo Dog, the Siberian Husky, and the Samoyed.

A husky is a sled dog used in the polar regions. One can differentiate huskies from other dog types by their fast pulling-style. They represent an ever-changing crossbreed of the fastest dogs. Humans use huskies in sled-dog racing. Huskies are also kept as pets, and groups work to find new pet homes for retired racing and adventure-trekking dogs.

Chihuahuas are small puppies with a round head and short snout. It has large round eyes, almost black in dark, sometimes dark ruby red. The distinctive big ears are always set. The Tibetan Mastiff is a large Tibetan dog breed belonging to the mastiff family. Its double coat is long, subject to climate, and found in a wide variety of colors, including solid black, black and tan, various shades of red and bluish-gray, often with white and blue markings.

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare . The beagle is intelligent. It is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
